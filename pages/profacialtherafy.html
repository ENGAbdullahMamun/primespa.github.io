ProFractional Therapy

Description

ProFractional Therapy with the Sciton laser offers dramatic results with minimal downtime. ProFractional ablates narrow-diameter channels into the skin, covering a fraction of the total treatment area. This action initiates the body’s wound healing response, while leaving the surrounding tissue intact for rapid healing.

ProFractional Therapy works very well as a stand-alone procedure to treat acne scars, freckles, brown spots, and wrinkling to reveal smoother, softer skin with a healthier luster. It can also be used in combination with other treatments for even more dramatic changes to the skin. Some examples of combination therapies include:

MicroLaserPeel™ + ProFractional – Start with Sciton’s MicroLaserPeel for full field treatment to remove superficial irregularities followed by ProFractional to stimulate collagen remodeling through deep fractional ablation.

BBL™ + ProFractional – Start with Sciton’s BBL for full field treatment to remove pigmented lesions followed by ProFractional.

ProFractional treatments are customized to meet the needs of each client. Tunable results can range from subtle to dramatic. Healing typically occurs within a few days and most clients usually feel comfortable enough to get back go work and apply makeup a couple days after treatment. Of course, downtime will vary depending on the aggressiveness of the treatment. Pricing depends on the recommended treatment to help each client achieve desired results. To learn which treatment option is right for you, we invite you to come in for a complimentary consultation with our Sciton laser technician.
